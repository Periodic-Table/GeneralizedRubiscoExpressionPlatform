Welcome to the HARVEST dry lab platform

HARVEST is a High-throughput Assay for Rubisco Variants via E. coli Screening and Testing. HARVEST was developed on the premise that advancing the frontier of land plant Rubisco research relies on the availability of a much higher throughput expression platform for wild-type and mutant Rubisco proteins.

Research on land plants, particularly when it comes to Rubisco, is notoriously slow. This is because, until now, the chassis for producing Rubisco proteins has necessarily been plants. HARVEST seeks to change that by developing a generalized Rubisco expression platform using E. coli. This page contains the beginnings of this research, a series of dry lab tests which seek to confirm the viability of the platform ahead of in vitro testing.
